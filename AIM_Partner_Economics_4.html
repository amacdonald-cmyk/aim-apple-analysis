Methodology Notes
1.Zinio net = gross Zinio revenue minus conversion fees, delivery fees, credit card fees, and license fees. Monthly average based on Jan–Apr 2026 invoice data.
2.AIM Apple = net Apple iTunes revenue for AIM-operated apps (via Zinio Pro platform). Excludes Taunton-entity apps (Fine Woodworking, Fine Homebuilding, Fine Gardening, Threads) — those are billed separately and not included in AIM's Zinio invoice.
3.Pro exit scenario assumes: license fee eliminated, Pro delivery fees eliminated, AIM Apple revenue retained for Keep titles only. Exited titles remain on Zinio Store + Library distribution.
4.Residual losses post-exit (Banknote Reporter, World Coin News, Numismatic News) are driven by content conversion fees against thin distribution revenue. Separate discussion needed on whether those titles remain on Zinio at all.
5.eMags migration scenario not modeled here — pricing proposal pending. That scenario would affect the Store + Library cost structure for exited titles.
