Methodology Notes
1.Pro fee schedule: 12 original titles per the Publisher Services Agreement v5.1 (March 2023) with confirmed billing rates. 4 Taunton titles (Fine Woodworking $300, Fine Homebuilding $300, Fine Gardening $300, Threads Magazine $200) added by Contract Amendment Order dated April 25, 2024. Total monthly Pro fees: $3,800 across all 16 titles.
2.Apple revenue = net receipts from the Jan–Apr 2026 digital revenue report, divided by 4 for monthly average. Taunton titles are billed through Cruz Bay Publishing in Apple's system but appear in AIM's revenue reporting and are on AIM's Zinio Pro invoice. Apple's ~30% platform fee has already been deducted.
3.Zinio Store + Library net/mo = estimated monthly net from Zinio distribution channels after conversion fees, delivery fees, and credit card fees, excluding the Pro license fee. The Pro fee is shown separately and removed in the scenario column.
4.4 titles with residual losses post-exit (Kovels, Numismatic News, World Coin News, Banknote Reporter) are driven by content conversion fees against thin revenue — not the Pro fee. These require a separate decision on whether Zinio distribution is viable for them at all.
5.eMags migration scenario not modeled — pricing proposal pending. Would affect Store + Library cost structure for all titles.
