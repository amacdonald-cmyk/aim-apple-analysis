Methodology Notes
1.Pro fee schedule sourced directly from confirmed billing data. 12 titles on Pro: Kovels Antique Trader, Cuisine at Home, Popular Woodworking, Writer's Digest, Garden Gate, Numismatic News, Old House Journal, World Coin News, Banknote Reporter, Woodsmith, Horticulture, Log & Timber Home Living. ShopNotes is not on the Pro schedule and is excluded.
2.Apple revenue = AIM entity net receipts from Jan–Apr 2026 digital revenue report, divided by 4 for monthly average. Excludes Taunton titles (Fine Woodworking, Fine Homebuilding, Fine Gardening, Threads) which are billed under Cruz Bay Publishing and covered separately in the Zinio Pro Analysis document.
3.Zinio Store + Library net/mo = estimated monthly net from Zinio distribution channels after conversion fees, delivery fees, and credit card fees. This figure includes the Pro fee drag in the current state column; removing the Pro fee is the only change in the scenario column.
4.Residual losses post-exit (Kovels, Numismatic News, World Coin News, Banknote Reporter) are conversion-fee-driven — not Pro-fee-driven. Those titles require a separate conversation about whether Zinio distribution is viable at all.
5.eMags migration scenario not modeled — pricing proposal pending. Would affect the Store + Library cost structure for all titles.
